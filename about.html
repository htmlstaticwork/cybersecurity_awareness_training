// OUR MISSION

Eliminating the Human Vulnerability

Cybercriminals don't break in — they walk in. 95% of all cyber incidents trace back to human error. Sophisticated technology can't stop an employee who doesn't know the warning signs.

CyberShield was built to change that. We use behavioral science, AI, and gamification to transform every employee — from the CEO to the intern — into a vigilant, security-conscious participant in your defense.
